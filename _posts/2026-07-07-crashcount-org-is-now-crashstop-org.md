title: Crash Count Chicago is now Crash Stop Chicago (crashstop.org)
---

A few days after I soft-launched "Crash Count Chicago", I stumbled on the excellent [CrashCount.nyc](https://crashcount.nyc/) site, which launched just a couple months earlier and has much of the same mission and data focus as Crash Count Chicago (and is in many ways, a much more interesting site!)

Well, they got the name/branding first, so I brainstormed some alternative names and **Crash Stop Chicago** had the best sound to it and thematic overtone, so [crashstop.org](https://crashstop.org/chicago) it is!
